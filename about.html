Ciao, I am SERENA ISONE

I am a Product Owner, playing with digital at Adidas.

But above all, I am a fanatic learner.
As a child, I found the question "What do you want to do when you grow up?" quite odd. I was curious about many things, "Why should I choose only one?".
Fast forward 30 years later, I consider it my strength: not knowing what I wanted to do allowed me to be flexible and experiment with different roles, live in a bunch of amazing countries and meet people who influenced my personal and professional growth in countless ways.

My curiosity recently drove me to Software Development: I am already appreciating the benefits of this new skill set in my current role.

The key is to stay curious and welcome the opportunities my interests guide me towards.
